Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How do I book an appointment? +
You can book an appointment online through our website by logging into your account and selecting your preferred treatment, doctor, and time slot. Alternatively, you can call us at +94 11 234 5679 during business hours.
What should I bring to my first consultation? +
Please bring any previous medical records, a list of current medications, your NIC or identification, and arrive 15 minutes early to complete any necessary paperwork.
Do you accept insurance? +
We work with most major insurance providers. Please contact your insurance company to verify coverage for Ayurvedic treatments, or call our office for assistance with insurance-related questions.
Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment? +
Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment through your patient dashboard or by calling us. We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations to avoid cancellation fees.
Are consultations available in languages other than English? +
Yes, our doctors are fluent in Sinhala, Tamil, and English. You can specify your language preference when booking your appointment.
